Our Story

Founded in 2012 by Johnny Hockin after 7 years at MTV Canada, Juniper Island brings broadcast-quality storytelling to digital content. From our early days as Red Bull Canada's exclusive music content partner to creating viral campaigns for major brands, we've built a reputation for exceptional video that delivers results.

Our work spans from 10-million-view viral campaigns to intimate documentaries, always with the same commitment to craft and creativity.

Our Approach

We believe great video starts with great stories. Our process is built on deep collaboration, creative excellence, and meticulous attention to detail. We're not just video producers – we're strategic partners who understand how to create content that moves audiences and drives business results.

With over 300 projects completed and 25 million+ views generated, we've proven that authentic storytelling and technical excellence are the keys to video success.
